HOW TO APPLY FOR RADIO

Radio Station submission

Adding your radio station to all1Zed live radio is simple. It takes a few steps which usually take a day for processing and regulatory authentication. When your radio station is added, you will receive an all1Zed notification with your live radio direct link. For more information call us on +260 762 888 000.

Submission Guidelines

The radio station stream must be online 24/7. The station must be licensed and available for global listeners. The preferable stream format for submission is M3U, MP3, PLS or AAC.
